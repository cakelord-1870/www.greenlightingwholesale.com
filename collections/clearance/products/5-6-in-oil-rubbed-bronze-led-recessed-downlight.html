NICOR’s DLR56 SELECT is the latest 5/6-inch LED Downlight with selectable color temperature options at 3000K, 4000K, and 5000K. The 800 Lumen DLR56 SELECT is the perfect solution for high-efficiency LED Lighting in Residential or Commercial settings. Whether you’re in the construction phase, or updating your existing recessed lighting, the DLR56 SELECT fits any 5-inch or 6-inch recessed housing to create a designer look. The new low-profile design now fits within recessed shallow housings while remaining incredibly easy to install. Utilizing the latest in LED technology, the DLR56 SELECT produces over 800 lumens of light output while drawing only 12.7-Watts of electricity. The DLR56 SELECT features an impressive CRI rating over 91, with an R9 Value of 51 creating an extraordinary light output quality. This means that your reds will be brighter, and your blues will be truer, regardless of the color temperature selected. The DLR56 SELECT includes a 120-Volt LED Driver with built-in dimming that exceeds 54,000 hours of use. The DLR56 SELECT includes an IDEAL Quick-Connect along with an Edison Base adapter to make installation as easy as possible. This all-in-one kit includes everything you need to upgrade your new or existing 5-inch or 6-inch recessed housing. No other parts or components are required. The DLR56 SELECT is Energy Star rated, Insulation-Contact rated, Wet-Location rated, and Airtight. It’s perfect for use in kitchens, living rooms, hallways, bathrooms, and showers. The DLR56 SELECT is protected by NICOR's 5-year limited warranty.
