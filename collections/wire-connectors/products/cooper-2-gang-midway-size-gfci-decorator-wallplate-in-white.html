Cooper 2-Gang Midway-Size GFCI Decorator Wallplate in White

Product Description
Cooper Wiring Arrow Hart™ 2-Gang GFCI Decorator wallplate in white color features thermoplastic construction for enhanced durability. It offers an elegant high-gloss finish to your decor. Midway-size wallplate has 4.940 Inch x 4.880 Inch cutout for easy connectivity. It has a screw-catch feature that accommodates mounting screws in place. Impact resistant wallplate withstands bowing and can be flush-fitted to offer convenient installation. Decorator wallplate is compatible with GFCI devices.
