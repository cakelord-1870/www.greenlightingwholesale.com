LED Edge-Lit Aluminum Housing Single Face Green Letters,BB

Part: FHEX24 A S G EM
Product Description

Product Description

 

The green edge lit LED exit sign surface mount features a surface housing application that can be mounted universally. Its sleek acrylic panel is available either double or single sided with an aluminum surface mount housing. This unit is ideal for commercial offices, retail stores, and apartment buildings.

While providing an eye-catching design, the glowing acrylic panel is fully compliant and perfect for the interior. Its panel is a high-polish, injection-molded and is ultrasonically welded to eliminate visible hardware.
Standard measurements for width, height, and depth. green lettering have graduated depth of molded letter, which provides uniform distribution on graphics.

Requires no maintenance and includes 6V sealed lead-acid battery and can use dual voltage 120V/277V.

In addition to its attractive appeal, the green edge lit LED exit sign surface mount is UL 924 Listed and fully compliant throughout the United States. It's capable of being applied as a universal mount to either any ceiling (top) or wall (back). This model also has a 90-minute battery backup, which allows for visibility during any power outage. 

This singled and single sided unit features a mirror insert that gives the panel a clear optical illusion. It also operates singled 120 or 277V AC power, and is damp location rated. Includes field applied chevron directional indicators, LED charge indicator, comes in AC and EM models.

Features


• Attractive sleek look, acrylic panel, aluminum housing
• Push to test button
• UL 924 Listed
• Dual Voltage 120/277V
• Long Life Energy Saving LED Light Source
• Emergency Backup
• Maintenance Free, 6V Sealed Lead-Acid Battery
• Mirror Divider between panel
• 90 minute Emergency Operation
• LED charge indicator
• Field applied chevron directional indicators

Application


The green edge recessed lit LED exit sign is specifically designed for interior commercial building use. It is a preferred choice for its design and surface mounting capability. It requires 120VAC/277VAC lamp wattage for its LED lamp.
